The Starks

Eddard Stark is the husband of Catelyn Stark of House Tully. He is the father of Robb, Sansa, Bran, Arya, Rickon Stark, and Jon Snow, his bastard son who lives at Winterfell. Ned is the son of Lord Rickard and Lady Lyarra Stark.

Starks traditionally have long faces with a lean build, dark hair, and grey eyes. As descendents of the First Men, the Starks worship the old gods and have been seated at Winterfell since it was raised by Bran the Builder, who founded House Stark and built the Wall. The crypts below Winterfell house the statues and remains of all past lords of Winterfell.

The houses sworn to House Stark include:
  • House Reed
  • House Glover
  • House Mormont
  • House Umber
  • House Manderly
  • House Flint
  • House Wull
  • House Norrey
  • House Liddle
  • House Tully
  • House Westerling
