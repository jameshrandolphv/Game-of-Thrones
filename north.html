Winter is coming!

Lord Eddard Stark is Warden of the North, which is a sparsely populated region that is roughly a third of the land mass of Westeros. This region is bounded by the Sunset Sea to the west and the Shivering Sea to the east.

Eddard's seat is Winterfell, located in the center of the north and southwest of the wolfswood, which is the largest forest in the Seven Kingdoms.

It is not uncommon for thousands to die in the North during its long, harsh winters. Snows can reach depths of 40 ft, and snowfalls can even occur during the summer.

The northmen are descendents of the First Men, and their incomes come primarily from the trading of wool, hides, timer, and silver.
